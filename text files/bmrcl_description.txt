<p><span style="font-size: 14px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Namma Bangalore, is a metro system for the city of Bangalore, Karnataka, India, a Special Project Venture of the Government of India and the Government of Karnataka.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14px;">Phase I comprises two lines spanning a length of 42.30 kilometres, of which about 8.82 kilometres is underground and about 33.48 kilometres is elevated.&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">There are 40 stations in Phase I, of which 7 stations are underground, 2 at grade and 31 are elevated.</span></p>
