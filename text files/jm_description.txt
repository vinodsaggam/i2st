<p><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 14px;">Jaipur Metro is a rapid transit system in the city of Jaipur, Rajasthan, India.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Construction on the mostly elevated part of the first line, called Phase 1A. Jaipur Metro began commercial service between Chandpole and Mansarovar.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">The Jaipur Metro Rail system is India&#39;s sixth metro rail system after those in Kolkata, Delhi NCR, Bangalore, Gurgaon and Mumbai.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 14px;">&nbsp;It is also touted as one of the fastest built metro systems in India.Jaipur Metro is the first metro in India to run on Double-storey elevated road and Metro track.</span></p>
