<p><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 14px;">Metro I is part of the metro system for the city of Mumbai, India. consists of 12 stations from Versova to Ghatkopar.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">The line connects the eastern and western suburbs of Mumbai.It is operated by the Mumbai Metro One Pvt Ltd (MMOPL).&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 14px;">The MMOPL is a joint venture company owned by Reliance Infrastructure, Veolia Transport and the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA).</span></p>
