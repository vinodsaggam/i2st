<p><span style="font-size: 14px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">The Doha Metro is a planned rapid transit system in Qatar&#39;s capital city that is scheduled to become operational by the end of 2019.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14px;">It will have four lines with an approximate overall length of 300 km and 100 stations.&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">It will be an integral component of the larger Qatar Rail network, which will include a long-distance rail for passengers and freight, linking Qatar to the GCC, and Lusail&#39;s city local light rail transit.</span></p>
